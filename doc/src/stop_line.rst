Stop Line
~~~~~~~~~

Stop line road mark.

.. hint::

  - Always on the road
  - White
  - Perpendicular to a traffic flow

..
  In the GIS Data Collection Specification the closest class is TYP_CD: J
  This continuous white line is used along expressway adjacent to paved shoulder and also as STOP LINES
  However in the etalon shapefile TYP_CD is M
  https://github.com/Olyseus/features_extraction_tool/issues/28#issuecomment-1039888502

.. admonition:: GIS Data Collection Specification

  - 16.22 Lane Marking, TYP_CD: M
