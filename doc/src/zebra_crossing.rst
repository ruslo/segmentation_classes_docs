Zebra Crossing
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Longitudinal white stripes road marks show the designated location for
pedestrians/cyclists to cross the road.

.. hint::

  - Always on the road
  - Parallel to the flow of the traffic

.. admonition:: GIS Data Collection Specification

  - 16.28 Road Crossing, TYP_CD: H, L, R

.. seealso::

  - :doc:`Pedestrian Crossing </src/pedestrian_crossing>`
